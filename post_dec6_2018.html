Succubus Heart 4.5 -SSE- Removed BDSM 'Type' that was determined by highest rating (opt-ing for a new scaling system) -SSE- Changed Stacks no longer have a cap, instead certain passives will 'consume' stacks when they take effect -SSE- Changed Stack Cap Level now increases "Consumable Stacks" which change how many can be consumed at once (for passives) -SSE- Changed Stacks no longer effect BDSM True Values -SSE- Changed Masochist Passive now reduces Health Cap by 5 and scales with Body Rating -SSE- Changed Violence Passive now reduces Status Cap by 10 and scales with Soul Rating -SSE- Changed Regal Glow Passive now reduces Magicka Cap by 5 and scales with Mind Rating -SSE- Changed Saving Essence now scales with Body Rating -SSE- Changed Luscious Liquides now Decreases Health/Magicka Cap and scales with Desire Rating -SSE- Changed Formal Beauty now consumes stacks when entering combat, recovery status based on stacks consumed (Replaces the old Passive) -SSE- Changed Force Master now reduces All Status Caps by 25 and decreases Force Required for SP by 10% per-rank (Caps at 90%) -SSE- Changed Devious Partner (Conversion) now scales with Mind Rating -SSE- Changed Healthy Absorber now decreases All Status Caps by 5 (same effect) -SSE- Changed Have Orgasm perk to increase Stamina Cap by 2 per-rank -SSE- Changed Absorb Status perk to increase Magicka Cap by 2 per-rank -SSE- Added BDSM True Values are now easily viewable in the Passives MCM (was previously burried in help text) -SSE- Added Soul Pets now lose 25% of thier XP on death -SEE- Fixed Combat Seduction should no longer trigger when settings are set to zero. -SSE- Fixed Drain Kill should no longer kill NPCs when the player isn't involved -SSE- Fixed Skill Levels MCM showing wrong "To Next" value when earning SP -SSE- Fixed Combat Seduction triggering from Healing Spells
