Personal Bio
Michael Stanich is the name, Art is the trade. I am a fun loving creative from Spokane Washington who loves nothing more than
to create. I'm described as reserved yet somehow sporadic with my diverse thought patterns and anyone that knows me well knows to never take me seriously, as I always try and bring humor into
the mix. When it comes to creation I work in everything from your standard Design and Illustrations while also diving quite deep into video creation, voice acting/commentary, deep analysis, web design, game design, code in several language, and if anything else pops up with creative possibilities I'll most likely try it as well!
With a passion for art and a strong desire to constantly improve I offer not only strong and unique creative works but back it up with my own unique personality. I'm incredibly social so if you have any questions make sure to hit me up on Twitter, join our public discord, or shout out using any other form of media you can find me around, I'd love to hear from you!
